# 🌑 DUST & DROSS: A Pocket RPG

Welcome to the **Endless Dusk**. In this world, the horizon is permanently bruised purple, and the only thing cheaper than life is a rusty sword.

---

## 🌎 The World: The Gilded Wastes
The world of *Dust & Dross* is a sprawling graveyard of a once-great empire. 

* **The Static Sun:** It hangs forever at the western edge, never setting, casting long, hungry shadows.
* **The Iron Rot:** Metal is alive here. If you don't oil your blade with salt-water daily, it might start whispering secrets you don't want to hear.
* **The Great Dross:** Massive piles of magical refuse that prospectors (like you) scavenge for "Spark," the only remaining fuel for civilization.

---

## ⚔️ Player Classes
Choose your burden. Each class starts with one unique **Gift**.

| Class | Description | Starting Gift |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Scrap-Shaman** | Speaks to the spirits of broken machines. | **System Shock:** Can "wake up" a piece of junk for 1D6 minutes. |
| **The Hollow Knight** | A warrior whose armor is grafted to their skin. | **Iron Skin:** Ignore the first 2 points of physical damage. |
| **The Veil-Walker** | Someone who has died once and didn't like it. | **Ghost-Step:** Can walk through thin walls once per day. |

## 🛠️ Armory & Gear
Weapons are categorized by how much "Rot" they have accumulated.

### Melee Weapons
1.  **Sharded Greatsword:** Heavy, jagged, and prone to breaking. Deals $2D8$ damage.
2.  **Vibro-Shiv:** A small needle that vibrates at high frequencies. Ignores armor but breaks on a roll of 1.
3.  **The Lead Pipe:** Reliable. Heavy. Doesn't talk back.

### Ranged Weapons
* **Crank-Pistol:** Requires a full turn to wind up. 
* **Bolt-Slinger:** Silent, deadly, but the bolts are expensive.

## 🎲 Core Mechanics
*Dust & Dross* uses a simple **D20** system.

1.  **The Check:** To do something difficult, roll a $1D20 + \text{Attribute}$.
2.  **Difficulty Classes (DC):**
    * *Easy:* 10
    * *Hard:* 15
    * *Impossible:* 20
3.  **The Price of Failure:** If you fail a roll by more than 5, your weapon gains **1 Point of Rot**. At 5 points, the weapon turns into a hostile metal spider and attacks you.

> **GM Tip:** Don't let them rest. The shadows in the Gilded Wastes grow longer the longer the players stay in one spot.
